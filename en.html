Before you sign, make sure your choice is right

Replacing or installing doors and windows is a significant investment for your home. Quotes that seem equivalent may have technical differences or gaps that cost thousands of euros. My goal is to provide you with independent technical guidance so that you can make an informed and secure decision.

Who is this for

The service is intended for individuals who:

  • Have received 1–3 doors & windows quotes
  • Want to compare details objectively
  • Wish to avoid unnecessary costs and technical failures
  • Are seeking an independent opinion before committing financially

I do not sell doors & windows

I do not promote specific companies

Evaluation is made strictly on technical data and field experience.

What the guidance includes

Depending on the package you choose, the following is provided:

  • Analysis and review of quotes
  • Comparison of 2–3 quotes where necessary
  • Highlighting potential technical or financial risks
  • Written evaluation (PDF) with clear recommendations
  • Phone consultation for clarifications
  • Guidance before the final signature
  • On-site inspection and monitoring until project delivery

My goal is not to reject a company, but to ensure that your choice is correct, safe, and well-documented.

5 traps in doors & windows quotes

1

Comparison based on price

Two quotes might have a large price difference without its origin being clear. Often, products with entirely different characteristics are compared without this being clearly stated.

2

Vague system description

Terms like thermal break or reinforced profile don't always mean the same thing. When the quote doesn't state specific features, it's hard to know what one is actually buying.

3

Lack of installation details

Proper installation plays a crucial role in the operation and lifespan of the doors and windows. In many quotes it is mentioned as "we installed" with a cost but without further clarification.

4

Additional costs that appear along the way

Transportation, crane usage, dismantling, minor repairs, or special tasks are not included in the initial quote. The final cost may experience a significant shift.

5

Decision without a second thought

When it comes to a large investment, a second independent opinion can prevent mistakes and unnecessary expenses.

Every project is different and there is no one-size-fits-all solution. Before proceeding, it is worth knowing if the quote you have in your hands truly meets the needs of your home.

If you already have a quote or are in the selection stage, you can ask for guidance before making your final decision.
